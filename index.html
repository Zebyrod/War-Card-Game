I Declare War!

Rules of War:

  • Be the first player to win all cards or take all of your opponents cards
  • Cards are divided evenly between players and placed face down
  • Players each draw a card face-up, the player with the higher value card keeps both cards drawn
  • If the cards match in value, IT IS TIME FOR WAR!... Both players will draw 3 cards each and select one to face up. The player with the higher value card takes all cards drawn(If cards are equal value again repeat until a player wins the faceoff)

Instructions

  • Click Play to begin
  • When it is your turn, click draw card
  • When It is time for War select a card you want to face-up
  • Continue until the game ends. And press Play Again for another round!
