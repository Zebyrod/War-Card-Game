War! The Card Game

Created by Zebastian Rodriguez

    Rules of War:
  • Be the first player to win all cards or take all of your opponents cards
  • Cards are divided evenly between players and placed face down
  • Players each draw a card face-up, the player with the higher value card keeps both cards drawn
  • If the cards match in value, IT IS TIME FOR WAR!
  • Both players will draw 3 cards each and select one to face up. The player with the higher value card takes all cards drawn
    Instructions:
  • Click draw card to flip cards
  • When It is time for War select a card you want to face-up
